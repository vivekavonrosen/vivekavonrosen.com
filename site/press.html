Official Bio

The short version

Viveka von Rosen founded Linked Into Business in 2006 and co-founded Vengreso in 2017, which scaled into a Fortune 500 digital sales training company. A LinkedIn member since 2004 and the author of eight bestselling books — including LinkedIn Marketing: An Hour a Day — she has been named to Forbes' Top Ten Most Influential Women on Social Media and published in Forbes, Fast Company, and Money Magazine. Today she works with experienced women founders, turning twenty-plus years of earned expertise into visible authority using LinkedIn, Substack, and AI.
